Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Information We Collect

Run It UP! Dallas collects your email address, display name, profile photo, pace group preference, and run day preferences when you create an account. We also collect check-in data, messages sent in group channels, and event RSVP information.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide the Run It UP! app experience — including check-ins, community chat, buddy matching, leaderboards, and event coordination. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (cloud infrastructure). Profile photos and shared images are stored in encrypted cloud storage.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your Profile settings. You can also edit your profile information at any time.
